Summary of Built-in Format Validators:
Type: Validates data type.
Required: Ensures presence of a value.
Min/Max: Numeric range validation.
MinLength/MaxLength: String length validation.
Match: Regex-based validation.
Enum: Restricts values to a set of options.
Unique: Ensures uniqueness at the database level.
